Account

Ever wanted a fully customizable Discord bot - for free?

Well, you have to vote for this bot. Easily doable with a few active members though :D



TomatenKuchen custom branding allows you to...

  • Change the username, avatar and About Me of the bot
  • Be able to use most of the TomatenKuchen features without any changes
  • Invite your custom branded bot to multiple servers
  • Remove the main TomatenKuchen bot from your server


"Pricing"

You pay for your custom bot(s) using credits. You can earn them by voting for TomatenKuchen and Manage Bot (a bot for managing Pterodactyl servers on Discord), by using the economy system of TomatenKuchen and by participating in giveaways and events on the TomatenKuchen Discord server.
