Account

Ever wanted a fully customizable Discord bot - for free?

Well, you have to vote for this bot. Easily doable with a few active members though :D



TomatenKuchen custom branding allows you to...

  • Change the username, avatar and About Me of the bot
  • Still be able to use most of the TomatenKuchen features without any changes
  • Remove the main TomatenKuchen bot from your server


"Pricing"

You pay for your custom bot(s) using credits. You can earn them by voting for TomatenKuchen and Manage Bot, a bot for managing Pterodactyl servers, and by using the economy system of TomatenKuchen.
